About the game

Every cat deserves the
perfect nap spot.

In Purrfect Fit, you arrange cozy spaces — beds, shelves, windowsills, and more — to satisfy a parade of fussy feline residents. Each level is a new puzzle: figure out where each cat wants to curl up, and earn their approval one purr at a time.

No timers. No fail states. Just cats, cozy vibes, and the deep satisfaction of everything fitting perfectly into place. The kind of game you can play for five minutes or five hours.
