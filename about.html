We make games

Blue Variable is a two-person indie studio. We make small, cozy games with big hearts — no crunch, no bloat, just craft.

Our mission

Thoughtful games,
made with care.

We started Blue Variable because we wanted to make the kind of games that feel like a warm cup of tea — inviting, calming, and just the right amount of engaging.

Our debut title, Purrfect Fit, is a cozy puzzle game launching in 2026. It's the kind of game you can pick up for five minutes or five hours — no pressure, just play.

We believe the best games come from a place of genuine love for the medium, not from chasing trends or hitting metrics. Blue Variable is our small bet that cozy, well-crafted experiences still matter.
